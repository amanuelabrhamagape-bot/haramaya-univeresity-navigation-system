About Campus Navigator

Your comprehensive guide to campus exploration

Our Mission

Campus Navigator is designed to help students, faculty, and visitors easily navigate campus. We provide an interactive, user-friendly platform that makes finding buildings, facilities, and services simple and efficient.

About This Project

This interactive campus navigation system was built to provide a modern, GIS-like experience without requiring backend infrastructure or APIs. It demonstrates how powerful web technologies can create engaging, functional applications that work entirely in the browser.

What We Offer

  • Interactive campus map with clickable buildings
  • Detailed information about each location
  • Smart search functionality
  • Direction finding between locations
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • No installation required - works in your browser
  • Real-time location information
  • Contact details for all departments
